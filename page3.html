About Me

Matt McColeman is a Software Developer with a B.Sc. Computer Science. He is Currently a Developer at TD Bank. He Gained experience working as a co-op student at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology as part of a team to deploy and upgrade existing software. The co-op position developed his strong research, problem solving and communication skills. These traits helped him as a teaching assistant leading and instructing students. Matt researched in Ontario Tech University's Visual Computing Research Lab. He wrote his honors thesis on AI. Matt has a great combination of education and work experience.
